Process

How I move from a fuzzy problem to a considered, tested solution.

01 Discover I start by understanding the problem space — talking to users, mapping systems, and identifying the assumptions worth challenging before anything is designed.
02 Define Research gets synthesised into a clear point of view: a problem statement, opportunity areas, and constraints that keep the work grounded throughout.
03 Design From rough sketches to high-fidelity prototypes, I explore widely before committing — testing ideas early so the best ones survive into production.
04 Deliver Handoff isn't the end — I stay close through implementation, ensuring the intent of the design survives the build and measuring outcomes once live.
